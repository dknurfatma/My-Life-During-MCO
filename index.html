In this page, I would like to share how to make wanton. I found this recipe on facebook and I will tell you how to do it here. Ok let's start.

Prepare the ingredients as listed below:

  • Chicken breast
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp 'maggi cukup rasa'
  • an egg
  1. First thing you need to do is to mince the chicken breast, carrot and onion until it become smooth.
  2. Then, mixed it all together includes sesame oil, white pepper, black pepper, 'maggi cukup rasa' and an egg. Beat until well mixed.
  3. Marinade for 10 minutes in order to flavour it.
  4. Prepare the dumpling skin and a small bowl of water as a glue to make wanton.
  5. After 10 minutes, using teaspoon, grab 1/2 the ingredients in the middle of the dumpling skin.
